About Us

Welcome to the Chess Club! We are a community of chess enthusiasts dedicated to promoting the game and fostering a love for strategy and competition.

Our club offers a variety of activities, including regular meetings, tournaments, and educational sessions for players of all levels.

Our Mission

To provide a welcoming environment for chess players to learn, compete, and connect with others who share their passion for the game.

Our History

Founded in 1995, the Chess Club has grown from a small group of local players to a vibrant community with members from all over the region.
